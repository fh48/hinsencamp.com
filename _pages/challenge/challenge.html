Who is this challenge for?

This challenge is for those Javascript-, frontend-, UI-, Web- Developers and Engineers who want to be considered a Senior. If you already had a couple years in the industry, this is your chance to make a jump in your career and excel in your field. In case you are just starting out and you want to see what's ahead, this challenge is also for you.

When it comes to advanced engineering topics, it's easy to feel paralyzed without a mentor. Where to start? Where to dig into next? Especially when you are comming out of a coding bootcamp it's tough to get a spin on all the stuff they haven't tought you. I am talking about thing like algorithms, Design Patterns or Data Structures. Let's change that! With this challenge we will level-up and grow senior together!

Why join?

  • Improve your coding skills to grow into a senior role.
  • Learn a fair share about neighbouring departments like Backend, UX/UI, Product management.
  • Create projects which prove you reached the senior level!
  • See others grow with you - Motivation boosts!
  • Learn to make technology desicions, choose the right tool for the job.
  • Apply advanced concepts like design patterns and complex data structures in real world projects.
  • Get in-depth tutorials which illustrate a solution approach.
  • It's FREE!

How does this challenge work?

Each Friday I send out the new challenge to all participants. The challenges focus on building all kinds of complex UI components, which require a good understanding of advanced concepts like data structures, design patterns, but also in-depth knowledge of web technologies (JavaScript, HTML, CSS), etc. to be implemented properly.

No worries though, I'll always provide an introduction to the very concept required to solve the challenge sucessfully. You can also expect a seletion of further learning resources. When it comes to frameworks I am not religious. A challenge can require to solve a problem in react, vue, or any of the others, or it up to you.

You can submit your implementation here(link) to share your result with the community. I will add all submissions (no matter when you submit) to the website. Please submit your code as a codeSandbox Not to forget, with each new challenge you receive an in-depth tutorial explaining an implementation of the previous weeks task. Most importantly, keep it fun!

Why am I hosting this challenge?

I believe programming skills should be affordable and open to all. Moreover, education material should teach real world skills and make theoretical concepts as easy to apply as possible. Everything else is a waste a student's valuable time. Moreover, with each submission I grow myself. Teaching and mentoring are two essential elements of in my professional and personal development.

Which technologies are covered?

Technology is always changing, that's why I follow an framework agnostic approach. You will see all kinds of technologies and develop an diverse set of transferable skills that will translate into you being adaptable.
